| T-59-38 | Information Disclosure / Integrity | public/dedicated split | mitigate | Catalog tests prove objects are physically in the configured schema. |
| T-59-39 | Denial of Service / Integrity | Oban redirection | mitigate | Do not add Oban references to library migrations; Oban remains host-owned. |
</threat_model>
