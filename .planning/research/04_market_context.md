# Market Context & Reference Architecture

## The Helpdesk Landscape
* **Standalone Giants (Zendesk, Help Scout)**: Feature-rich but operate as external silos.
* **Open Source Clones (Chatwoot, Papercups)**: Validate self-hosted support, but become monolithic standalone apps.
* **Modern API-First (Plain, Pylon)**: Emphasize API programmability and account context.

## The Cairnloop Wedge
Cairnloop's differentiator is being **embedded**. It competes on deep, native integration with the Phoenix host application. 

## Customer-Led Growth (CLG) & Voice Activation
Drawing from adjacent markets (Extole, Mention Me, AppFollow), Cairnloop recognizes that support is the gateway to growth.
* **The Insight**: B2C/B2B subscription apps need to identify happy users to trigger review asks, referrals, and UGC capture.
* **The Implementation**: Cairnloop will provide the *signals* (telemetry on positive sentiment shift after resolution) that the host application's growth marketing layers can act upon. It acts as the "Private Recovery" net for detractors and the "Satisfaction Trigger" for promoters.
