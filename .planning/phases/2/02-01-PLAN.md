Implement the Notifier behaviour and the optional Chimeway adapter to dispatch SLA breach events asynchronously to external channels.

Output: A new `Cairnloop.Notifier` behaviour, a `Chimeway` optional dependency, the built-in adapter, and integration into the `CheckSLA` worker.
</objective>
